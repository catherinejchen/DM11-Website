ANIMATE



BOUNCING BALLS


What?
Create an animation with balls that are bouncing off walls (surfaces) with the use of both motion tween, and no motion tween. Sound effects must also be added and a theme must be used. A clear frame is also necessary.

Software:

  • Animate (www.adobe.com)

Skills:

  • Implement a theme: Consider possible options for overarching theme and cater the entire assignment towards said theme.
  • Importing sound files: Learn how to put sound into flash.
  • Animating: Learn the different tools (motion tween etc.).

Thoughts?
I enjoyed coming up with a theme and carrying it out. However, animating without the help of motion tween takes a long time and adding sounds into flash is tedious in my opinion


ANIMATION


What?
Create your own cartoon character using graphics and individual movie clips for different body parts. Then, combine the movie clips and create a scene for your character and create a cool and creative animation.

Software:

  • Animate (www.adobe.com)

Skills:

  • Movie Clips: How movie clips work and how they are useful as well as how they are integrated into an animation.
  • Creativity: Design and create your own unique character with the tools available.
  • Planning: Seperating the body parts into movie clips accordingly.

Thoughts?
I find that animating characters are a lot of work and making them fluid takes a while. Getting used to digital art takes a while but I love having the opportunity to practice my digital art skills.
 
 


COLOURING BOOK


What?
Create a colour book with unique and sharp images (create your own). The colour book must have 2 pages and each page must have at least 10 different colour regions (*BONUS* Change the mouse cursor to be different).

Software:

  • Animate (www.adobe.com)

Skills:

  • Code: Learn the code required for the colouring book.
  • Creativity: Design your own character/scene for the colouring book.

Thoughts?
I enjoyed the digital art portion of this assignment and creating the scene. However, the coding took a lot of work trying to understand and implement but once I understood it, it became easy as it was just copy and paste from there onwards.
