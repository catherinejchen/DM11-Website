What?
Create an educational tutorial tutorial for an open source program. This assignment is dedicated to learning the tools in a graphic program and sharing the information that we learn. Also, this is a great opportunity to get used to the interface.

Software:

  • Gimp (www.gimp.org)
  • Windows Movie Maker(www.windows-movie-maker.org

Skills:

  • Creativity: Let your imagination run wild to design and produce a unique image of your choice.
  • Patience: Research different tools and techniques to achieve your goal
  • Editor: Crop and put together videos.

ONLINE PHOTO EDITING


What?
To use an online photo editing program and learn the different tools as well as how to share it remotely on Moodle (meaning that a file is not needed to be recopied or reuploaded). Also, we learn how to share photos through photo sharing websites.

Software:

  • Photoshop Express (www.photoshop.com/tools?wf=editor)
  • Photobucket (www.photobucket.com)

Skills:
  • Simple Online Photo Editing Tools: Learn about the different tools in Photoshop Express and how to apply them to photos to create your own flare
  • Uploading Photos and Sharing: Uploading photos onto Photobucket and changing the visibility settings so you can share it.
  • Creativity: Design your images


Thoughts?
A creative assignment that allows the student (me) to learn an easily accessible photo editing program that is online. I also get the change to learn about different tools such as dodge and burn and apply them. I found this extremely useful as I do not have photoshop and this would be a great way to add a blast of flavour into a simple photo.
