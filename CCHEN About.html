ONLINE CORKBOARD


What?
To create a virtual themed online corkboard that reflects who you are (hobbies, interests, skills etc.). Also, this assignment gives the opportunity for students to learn how to add flavour into a resume to show who you are.


Software:

  • Lino (en.linoit.com)

Skills:

  • Illustrate Creativity: Demonstrate your own personality in the assignment.
  • Organization: Grouping information together and placing it in an attractive manner.
  • Design: Create a visually appealing layout that captures the reader's attention.

Thoughts?
Fun and unique way to deliver information about myself and present myself in a more visually attractive form. This assignment is valuable to me as it give me the opportunity to learn another possible resume style (or a way to make my resume more interesting and eye catching). I am fond of creating Inspiration Boards, therefore I found this assignment extremely enjoyable.

DIGITAL PORTFOLIO


What?
Create a digital portfolio that relays a story of your personal work (can be from Digital Media 11 or another class). The final product should show your advancements and progress throughout the course as well as what you've learned.


Software:

  • IMovie (apple.com/ca/imovie/)
  • QuickTime Player (apple.com/quicktime)

Skills:

  • Files: Importing different types of files (png, mp4 etc.) into IMovie.
  • Editor: Editing different types of files and putting them into a video.
  • Video Edits: Using transitions and text tools.
  • Organization: Organizing your work and sorting them in a logical manner.

Thoughts?
This assignment acted as an overview of all the work I have done in this course. I enjoyed creating a timeline of my work and seeing the progress I have made as well as the gradual improvement in quality throughout the semester. I enjoyed learning the tools in IMovie and becoming familiar with the software. This is a useful assignment as being able to work with IMovie and edit videos will come in handy.
